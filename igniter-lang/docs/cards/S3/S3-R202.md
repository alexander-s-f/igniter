# S3-R202 - if_expr RuntimeSmoke Consumer Boundary Design

Status: planned
Date: 2026-05-28

Dispatch: R202 = C1-D -> C2-X -> C3-A -> C4-S

Pattern:
- C1-D designs whether and how `RuntimeSmoke` may consume the accepted proof
  RuntimeMachine `if_expr` path.
- C2-X pressure-checks the design for transitive-load, dual-path evaluator,
  public-claim, and closed-surface safety.
- C3-A accepts, conditionally accepts, holds, or redirects the design and
  decides whether a later implementation-authorization review may open.
- C4-S curates the outcome and exact next Main Line boundary.

Round intent:
- Move from accepted proof RuntimeMachine `if_expr` adapter consumption to a
  design-only RuntimeSmoke consumer boundary.
- Decide whether RuntimeSmoke may later recognize or exercise `if_expr` through
  the already accepted proof RuntimeMachine path.
- Explicitly handle the R201 transitive-load note: RuntimeSmoke loads
  `compiled_program.rb`, and `compiled_program.rb` now loads the evaluator.
- Decide whether the Slice 1 / Slice 2 dual-path evaluator shape must remain
  intact, be preserved as a known debt, or be redesigned before RuntimeSmoke
  work.
- Preserve all public/release/production/API/CLI/Spark/counterfactual/cache
  boundaries.

Current accepted state:

```text
R199:
  accepted Slice 1 live internal evaluator:
    IgniterLang::SemanticIRExpressionEvaluator
    supported kinds: literal, ref, if_expr
    direct-require-only, not root-required

R201:
  accepted Slice 2 proof RuntimeMachine consumer path:
    proof RuntimeMachine can consume evaluator through proof-only if_expr adapter
    PRT-IF1..PRT-IF15 PASS, 56/56
    external_evaluator: per-call hook accepted
    apply, field_access, tbackend_read remain proof RuntimeMachine-local
    RuntimeSmoke remains closed
    transitive RuntimeSmoke evaluator load recorded as known consequence only
```

Global constraints:

- Do not edit root require `igniter-lang/lib/igniter_lang.rb`.
- Do not edit `igniter-lang/lib/igniter_lang/semanticir_expression_evaluator.rb`.
- Do not edit `igniter-lang/experiments/runtime_machine_memory_proof/compiled_program.rb`.
- Do not edit `CompilerOrchestrator`, `CompilerResult`, `CompilationReport`,
  `Diagnostics`, parser, classifier, TypeChecker, SemanticIR emitter,
  compiler behavior, assembler, loader/report, CompatibilityReport, package or
  release files, public API/CLI, Spark, or production behavior.
- Do not authorize public demo, stable, production, all-grammar, public runtime,
  Spark, or API/CLI claims.
- Do not mutate accepted release evidence.
- Do not execute release commands.
- Do not publish/yank gems.
- Do not create/delete/push tags.
- Do not sign or deploy.
- Do not introduce dynamic selected-branch dependency tracking,
  path-sensitive dependency receipts, dynamic dependency authority,
  path-sensitive cache keys, cache invalidation changes, freshness changes, or

- Do not implement counterfactual audit, dry-runs, comparison reports, effect
  sandboxing, or public counterfactual API/CLI.
- Do not move `apply`, `field_access`, or `tbackend_read` into evaluator core.
