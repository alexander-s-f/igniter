## Superseded Or Not Canon

- `Igniter.interactive_app` as one broad immediate facade is not accepted canon.
- Flow/wizard/SSE/proactive-agent DSL proposals are product pressure, not current
  language or package API.
- UI Kit and Igniter Plane are not current frontend implementation targets.
- Runtime Cell manager, split/merge/replicate/cell marketplace, and cell gossip
  are research only.
- Semantic Gateway is not a required human-agent interaction layer.
- Product proposal catalogs are not roadmap commitments.
- P1-P5 delta estimates are historical planning pressure, not current schedule.
- Concept-mined vocabulary and Line-Up compression are not default handoff
  syntax.
- Expert report labels such as “implementation can begin immediately” are not
  supervisor approval.

## Values To Preserve

- **Evidence before action:** observe, inspect, review, then mutate.
- **Agents as participants:** agents should have state, evidence, capabilities,
  gates, and receipts rather than hiding behind provider calls.
- **Authoring compression:** strong primitives need a humane authoring surface.
- **Process compression:** active context must stay bounded; history remains
  reachable but cold.
- **Receipts as business artifacts:** incident, delivery, activation, clinical,
  scientific, and mission records are outputs, not logs.
- **Product pressure realism:** good apps solve real pain, run small first, and
  leave clear expansion paths.
- **Critical-domain honesty:** science, robotics, space, and medicine sharpen
  primitives but also expose when existing domain tools may be better.
- **Research graduation:** expert ideas become doctrine, read-only reports,
  package facades, or implementation only through explicit acceptance gates.
