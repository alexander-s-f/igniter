## Decision

Use PostgreSQL as the primary system of record for billing and customer data.

## Context

Billing workflows require strong consistency, transactional writes, and a clear
audit trail that support can inspect during customer disputes.

## Constraints

- Customer and billing data must remain transactional.
- Consistency is more important than flexible document shape.

## Rejected Options

- MongoDB: rejected because transaction and consistency limitations create
  unacceptable billing risk.
