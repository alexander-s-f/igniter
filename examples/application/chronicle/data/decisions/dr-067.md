## Decision

Keep personally identifiable information in relational storage unless security
and legal sign off on a narrow exception.

## Context

The legal team needs predictable retention, deletion, and export behavior for
customer profile fields.

## Constraints

- PII must remain auditable.
- Access patterns must support legal export and deletion requests.
- Security review is required before moving PII into a new data store.

## Rejected Options

- Document store for user profiles: rejected until legal export and deletion
  requirements are proven.
