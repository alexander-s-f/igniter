## Decision

Use Meilisearch for product catalog full-text search while keeping PostgreSQL as
the source of truth.

## Context

PostgreSQL full-text search was too slow for product catalog queries over two
million records.

## Constraints

- Search index may be eventually consistent.
- Product catalog writes still originate in PostgreSQL.
- Customer PII is not indexed.

## Rejected Options

- Elasticsearch: rejected because operational overhead was too high for the
  team at this scale.
